Roast Beef Sandwhich

A Roast Beef Sandwhich is that perfect lunchtime meal all year round.

Check out our great Roast Beef Sandwhich recipe to enjoy today!

Ingredients

  • Roast Beef
  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread
  2. Add mayo to one slice of bread
  3. Add roast beef
  4. Top off with Lettuce and Tomato
