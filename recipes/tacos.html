Tacos

Tacos are an easy to make home meal that can last you for days!

Check out our great Tacos recipt to enjoy today!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • 1 White Onion
  • Tortilla shells

Steps

  1. Dice and Sautee the Onion, and then set Onions aside
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Add 3/4 cup of water and taco seasoning to ground beef
  4. Add sauteed onions to ground beef
  5. Add cooked ingredients to tortilla
  6. Add sour cream and salsa
