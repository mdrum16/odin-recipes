Lasagna

Lasagna is greaty hearty meal for those cold winter nights.

Check out our great Lasagna recipe to enjoy today!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Riccotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  2. Heat up the Pasta Sauce
  3. Brown the ground beef, season to taste
  4. Layer the cooked Lasagna Noodles, Ground Beef, Riccotta Cheese, and Pasta Sauce in a baking dish
  5. Bake for 35 min at 400 degrees
